How to complete the ratings

{% if is_safeguarding %}

Apply a RAG self-rating to each Met judgement area. Any red means safeguarding cannot be judged Met.

{% else %}

Start with the Expected Standard statements and RAG each one:

  • Red — Insufficient evidence to meet the standard
  • Amber — Partial evidence; you believe the standard is met but some evidence is insecure.
  • Green — Comprehensive evidence supports your judgement.

You should use the supporting tool to help arrive at your judgement.

The next set of statements appears automatically based on your ratings: all green climbs to the next standard, an amber holds you where you are, and any red concludes Needs Attention. Your grade is concluded for you in the box at the bottom.
